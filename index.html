About Us

Founded by a determined young woman, Break the Silence emerged to address the silence surrounding mental health among youth in Kiambu.

We combine community support with innovative animal-assisted therapy through rabbit farming. Caring for rabbits calms the mind, restores routine, and rebuilds self-worth.

"Together, we turn silence into strength. We turn pain into power. We turn rabbits into real recovery."
