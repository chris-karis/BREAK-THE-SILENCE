Understanding Mental Health Challenges

Anxiety & Depression

Common mental health conditions that affect millions worldwide. Symptoms include persistent sadness, worry, and loss of interest in activities once enjoyed.

Effective treatments include therapy, medication, lifestyle changes, and peer support.

Trauma & Stress

Trauma can significantly impact mental health, especially in young people. Healing requires specialized support and trauma-informed care.

Our rabbit therapy program provides a gentle, non-judgmental environment for processing difficult emotions.

In Crisis? Get Help Now

If you're experiencing thoughts of suicide or are in emotional distress, free and confidential support is available 24/7 from the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

Call 988 Text 988 Chat Online

How to Access Mental Health Support

Steps to Get Support

  1. Recognize the signs and symptoms
  2. Reach out to someone you trust
  3. Contact a helpline or healthcare provider
  4. Explore treatment options that fit your needs
  5. Consider peer support groups

Payment & Access

Many resources are available regardless of ability to pay:

  • Sliding scale fees based on income
  • Insurance assistance programs
  • Community health centers
  • Free and low-cost clinics
